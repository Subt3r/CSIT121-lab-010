Why this standard matters

Paragraph 1.

The Web of Things Architecture 1.1 is important because is helps allow smart devices to communice while using common web technologies. This allows devices from even different compoanies work alongside one another more easily in smart homes, businesses, etc.

Paragraph 2.

The WoT standard improves interoperability, which means devices can share there information and services much more effeciently. It can also help developers with building Internet of Things (IoT) systems using technology they are familiar with like HTTP or JSON.

Paragraph 3.

Another feature I find to be important is its thing description, which allows devices to describe what they can do and how exactly other devices can interact with them. This then makes the smart systems much easier to then manage and automate things going forward.
